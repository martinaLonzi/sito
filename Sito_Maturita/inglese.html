CARBON FOOTPRINT

The project focuses on the carbon footprint, meaning the pollution caused by everyday human activities (transportation, energy use, food, waste). It analyzes the environmental effects such as global warming, biodiversity loss, and pollution. Finally, it proposes solutions to reduce environmental impact, including recycling, using sustainable transport, and solar energy. The key message is that everyone can help protect the planet through small daily actions.
